# EIC Motor Insurance Dashboard (2014–2018)

## Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analysing the financial performance of EIC, one of the most prominent motor insurers operating between 2014 and 2018. The report uncovers key trends in premiums, claims, policy growth, and risk patterns across vehicle types and usage categories.

## Key Metrics
- 📋 **508K** Policies Opened
- 💰 **$4.04B** Total Premium Collected
- 🚗 **$9.18B** Total Claims Paid
- 📊 **44%** Overall Premium-to-Claim Ratio
- 🔢 **38K** Total Claim Count

## Key Insights
- The premium-to-claim ratio surged dramatically in Q2 2018, reaching 2.2x — signalling a significant shift in claims activity
- **Motorcycles** led policy purchases (106K), yet most vehicle categories including Trucks, Pick-ups, and Automobiles recorded net claim losses
- **General Cartage** had the lowest claim ratio (27%), making it the most profitable usage segment
- **Toyota** vehicles accounted for the highest total claims at $2.59B, followed by Isuzu ($1.66B) and Iveco ($1.41B)
- **SEX 0** policyholders accounted for 63.39% of all claims, indicating a strong demographic risk skew
- Average claim amounts declined steadily from $22,917 in 2014 to $5,887 in 2018, suggesting improved risk management over time
- **Agricultural Own Farm** usage showed extreme volatility, peaking at an extraordinary claim ratio in 2018

## Tools Used
